Privacy Policy

Two Up Tracker is designed to keep your betting history private and under your control.

Effective Date: 1 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Two Up Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Two Up Tracker iOS app.

Information We Collect

Two Up Tracker stores the information you enter in the app, such as session names, rounds, bets, and related statistics.

We do not require account creation and we do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or location through the app.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide app functionality, including tracking sessions, calculating statistics, and displaying charts and history.

We do not sell your information and we do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud backup or device backup, your data may also be included in those backups under Apple's services and policies.

Third-Party Services

The app may be distributed through Apple services such as the App Store or TestFlight. Those services are governed by Apple's own privacy terms.

Data Retention and Deletion

You control your data. You can delete sessions within the app, or remove all app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Two Up Tracker is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date.
